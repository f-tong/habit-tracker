Welcome to the Habit Tracker!

This page was inspired by the book Atomic Habits by James Clear. One of his tips to making good habits is to make it satisfying, and one of the ways you can do this is by physically checking something off after you complete a habit. That's what this tracker is for! Go to the tracker page to input the habits you'd like to start and check them off each day. Happy tracking!
